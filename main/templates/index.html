YRON’S ALPHA
OVERVIEW
Holding one of the 1111 YRON’S ALPHA passes gives holders year-long access to a valuable suite of NFT-related tools, features, and alpha from experts designed to give NFT traders the competitive edge in such a rapidly moving market Holders will get to participate in exclusive whitelist giveaways, retain control of the community by guiding the direction of future benefits, and have first access to alpha fromYRON and his team. Members of the community who stand out will be rewarded with leadership roles and other benefits as the YRON’S ALPHA team works in tandem to build a community FOR the community.
YRON’S ALPHA GIVES YOU
NFT TRADING TOOLS
Holding a YRON’S ALPHA pass will give owners access to an advanced and highly set of tools integrated in the discord, including but not limited to real time mint alerts, smart money trade trackers, whale wallet trackers, listing bots, and more. The team is entirely open to adjusting tools available as the community sees fit.
WHITELIST SPOTS
YRON, his team and their friends in the NFT space will be constantly leveraging their rapidly growing network as prominent figures to acquire whitelist spots for the YRON’S ALPHA community. Each YRON’S ALPHA pass will act as one entry and this will be a large focus for the project.
RAPID ALPHA
YRON and his team, along with active and trusted members of the community, will bring NFT-related market information straight to you in real time. If you hold a YRON’S ALPHA pass, the team’s alpha is your alpha, and you will have first dibs on the gems that YRON has become notorious for finding in the NFT space
